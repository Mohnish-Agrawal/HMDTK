Introduction

Our goal is to spread awareness about your privacy, and inform you about what data a company collects from you and how this information can be used to exploit you.

Some of the companies profile us even when we don't use their platforms. The extent of how much they can profile us and how much they can predict is disturbing.

This is a one stop resource for all things related to privacy. Here, we will show you what data exactly a company collects from you, as simple as possible. You can also ask specific questions, and our ML model will answer them, based on that company's privacy policy. You can also sign up for a newsletter that will keep you updated of major changes in a company's privacy policy.

We have also developed a model that simulates how these companies profile their users. With just your search history, one can find out so much about you, and this information can be used to exploit you.

Summarizing privacy policies


We intend to keep you updated on any new changes made in the privacy policies by big tech giants. But it's really tiresome to read each and every policy document. We also know that data has slowly become the most valuable resource in this era.

So, we have made a system which can retrieve and summarize the whole policy document and answer the basic questions regarding Data privacy. These questions are:

  • The Data being collected by tech giants
  • Is our precious data being shared?
  • Am I being tracked online?
  • How is my data being used?
  • For how long is my data retained?

To get answers to the questions asked above, you can click on the cards below. If we have not already summarized a document that you are looking for, please upload your policy document in the card with "upload file" given below.


User profiling

Now that you know what all data is collected by these companies, one question remains -
How does this matter to me?

Your data the abstract portrait of who you are, and, more importantly, of who you are compared with other people is your real vulnerability. Not only your identity may get compromised online, but also it might change the way you interact with the internet

What these companies are doing is building little models, AKA, little avatars of you. Your avatar sits in the cloud, and they are trying to find what makes you use their product or service more, or which personalized advertisement of a third-party might make you buy its product. This is what we call "User Profiling"

One of the ways to profile users is to try and collect users' search history through cookies. We will not be getting into how this works. What we would really like to show you is the extent of information that can be gathered about you just by using your search history.

We have developed a system which can tell us about your interests. It's a very simplified model of what all big tech companies do. If you would like to see it work, you will have to provide us with your search history. And don't worry, all the data that you provide will be deleted once you leave this website.

Please follow the steps provided below to get your search history:

  • Add this extension to your browser - Export History/Bookmarks to JSON/CSV*/XLS*
  • You can view your extensions on top right corner of the screen. The icon looks like a puzzle
  • Click on the Export History/Bookmarks extension and then click on chrome history.json button
  • A file named chrome_history.json should be downloaded. You can upload this file by clicking on Profile me button given below
